About us

Owned and operated by a local South Korean family from Lynnwood, Yummy Bites offers a flavor packed menu ranging from Japanese fusion Teriyaki to traditional and trending Korean entrees. Located in the heart of University District, this warm and cozy restaurant will surely make you feel right at home the moment you walk in and have you leaving with a satisfied belly every time.
